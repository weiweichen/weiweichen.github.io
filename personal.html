Intro

I was born and grew up in Shanghai, in the southwestern area, the Xuhui District. I graduate from Nanyang Model High School and went to college in Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am a member of UCI sailing club, rated as capri skipper. I enjoy the breeze, sea animals, and beautiful view in the Newport harbor. I am a PADI licensed scuba diver since 2012.

I play harmonica, clarinet, and voilin sometimes, very amateur though. My current interest is classical acoustic guitar.

I have been selected by Google as one of the winners of the Glass Explorer program in summer 2013 and now a proud owner of Google Glass.
